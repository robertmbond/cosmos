Meetings

COSMOS meetings are open to faculty, graduate students, and collaborators who want to exchange ideas and connect across labs.

Weekly gathering

When: Wednesdays, 3:00–4:00 PM

Where: Kelley Engineering Center 1007 (hybrid)

Zoom: Link shared via mailing list

Format

  • 15-minute rotating updates from members.
  • Short research spotlight or guest presentation.
  • Planning for events, workshops, and shared initiatives.

Present or host

If you would like to share work-in-progress, host a speaker, or propose a discussion topic, reach out to the organizers.
