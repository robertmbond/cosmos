Oregon State University

Collaborative Space for Modeling, Optimization, and Systems Research.

COSMOS is a cross-lab research collective bringing together faculty and graduate students working across data-informed decision making, mathematical modeling, and applied systems.

Meet the group Weekly meetings

What we do

  • Share methods across labs and disciplines.
  • Host open discussions on new research and tools.
  • Coordinate events and mini-conferences.
  • Support students and collaborators.

Our focus areas

We aim to stay flexible and collaborative, without tying the group to a single lab or PI.

Decision analytics

Optimization, risk-aware planning, and scalable algorithms.

Systems & society

Engineering, public infrastructure, and human-centered systems design.

Data-driven modeling

Hybrid modeling, uncertainty quantification, and interpretable analytics.
