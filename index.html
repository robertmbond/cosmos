Ohio State University

Computational and Statistical Models Group.

We are a group of social scientists dedicated to advancing our understanding of individuals, relationships, communities, and society using computational and statistical tools. We conduct research that contributes to the development of new methodologies and the application of these tools to a wide range of social and communication issues. We are committed to thinking critically about our own work and exploring new directions for research.

What we do

  • Share methods across labs and disciplines.
  • Host open discussions on new research and tools.
  • Coordinate events and mini-conferences.
  • Support students and collaborators.

Our focus areas

Decision analytics

Optimization, risk-aware planning, and scalable algorithms.

Systems & society

Engineering, public infrastructure, and human-centered systems design.

Data-driven modeling

Hybrid modeling, uncertainty quantification, and interpretable analytics.
